# Skill: Final Trade Report

Loaded by the portfolio manager to format the final call. A **skill**: pure
output-structure guidance, editable by a non-engineer.

## Required structure

1. **Call:** one of `BUY` / `SELL` / `HOLD`, in bold, on the first line.
2. **Conviction:** 1–5, with one sentence on why that level.
3. **Thesis:** 2–3 sentences — the core reason, tied to the analysts' evidence.
4. **Key risks:** the 1–2 things most likely to make this call wrong.
5. **Invalidation:** the concrete condition under which the call should flip.

## Rules

- The call must follow logically from the risk-adjusted trade and the plan.
- Name the binding constraint from the risk debate.
- End with this exact line: `Educational illustration only — not investment advice.`
