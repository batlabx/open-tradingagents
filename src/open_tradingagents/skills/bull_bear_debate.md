# Skill: Bull / Bear Debate

Loaded by the bull and bear researchers. A **skill**, not a tool: it shapes how
they argue and has no side effects. Edit it to change debate quality without
touching code.

## How to argue well

- **Engage the other side.** Directly rebut the opponent's last point before
  adding a new one. A debate is not two monologues.
- **Ground every claim in the analyst reports.** Cite the specific number
  (price, RSI, margin, headline) that supports your case.
- **Steelman, then counter.** State the strongest version of the other side,
  then explain why your evidence outweighs it.
- **Stay falsifiable.** Say what would change your mind ("if revenue growth
  falls below X, the bull case breaks").

## What NOT to do

- Do not invent data that is not in the analyst reports.
- Do not repeat yourself across rounds — add new evidence or a new angle.
- Do not hedge into "it depends"; take a clear side. The judge needs signal.
